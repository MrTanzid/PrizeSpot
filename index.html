Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim my reward?

Simply click on the "Claim Reward" button for the offer you're interested in. You'll be directed to a page where you can complete the requirements to receive your reward. For iOS-exclusive offers, you must be using an iOS device.

How long does it take to receive my reward?

Most digital rewards (gift cards) are delivered within 24-48 hours via email. Physical items like product samples may take 1-2 weeks for delivery, depending on your location.

Are these rewards really free?

Yes! All rewards on PrizeSpot are completely free. Some offers may require you to complete a short survey or other requirements from our sponsors, but there is never any purchase necessary to receive your reward.

Why are some offers iOS exclusive?

Some of our premium partners specifically target iOS users for their marketing campaigns. These exclusive offers are only available to users on Apple devices. Don't worry though - we have plenty of great rewards for Android users too!

How many rewards can I claim?

You can claim as many different rewards as you like! However, each user is limited to one of each specific reward. This ensures everyone has a fair chance to enjoy our offers.
